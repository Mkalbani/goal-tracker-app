How to Use My Daily Goal Tracker

Welcome to the Goal Tracker!


Create goals by clicking the "+" button, then add a name and select an icon.


Delete goals by right-clicking or long-pressing on touch devices.


Track progress by clicking on a goal to mark it as completed.


Change themes using the theme switcher button.


Stay organized and motivated to achieve your goals!
